Our Memories

In our Department of Electronic Engineering, memories are gently built through care, respect, and shared moments. Behind each lesson and every project, there is the quiet guidance of teachers and the eager spirit of students.

Our teachers do more than teach. They inspire, they support, and they walk beside us. Their patience and wisdom shape our growth, both as learners and as people. We, the students, respond not just with effort, but with trust, thankfulness, and deep respect.

The bond between teacher and student here is honest and warm. We help one another, listen to each other, and celebrate together. From traditional ceremonies to lively events, we create memories that carry meaning and joy.

Among us, there is kindness. In every shared laugh, in every thoughtful word, and in every quiet gesture — we feel the spirit of our department. These moments remind us that we belong, not just to a place of learning, but to a community that truly cares.
