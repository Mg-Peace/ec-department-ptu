Department of Electronic Engineering

The Department of Electronic Engineering offers a range of programs leading toward Master of Engineering (M.E) degrees and Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) degrees. At present, the department has 13 full-time members. Total enrollments include (150) undergraduate students and (2) master students. Students are also doing the regular basic jobs and the special projects at the ends of each academic year. And then, the final year students are also doing the B.E mini thesis and going to industrial attachments.

Objectives

To carry out for getting 85% of Students’ satisfaction regarding to teaching and learning process in the analysis of Customer Satisfaction in Electronic Department for (2024-2025) Academic Year.

Our Mission

To nurture qualified electronic engineers who are skillful and are able to support with their related fields in future nation's electronic fields.

Our Vision

To produce electronic engineers who can keep abreast with ASEAN countries and who are not only theoretically but also practically skillful and outstanding in electronic fields.

Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • PEO 1: Graduates will be able to apply scientific and engineering principles to solve complex problems creatively and innovatively in research and design.
  • PEO 2: Graduates will be able to become professional engineers with effective communication, teamwork, and leadership skills relating to social and engineering challenges.
  • PEO 3: Graduates will demonstrate ethical behavior and professional competence in lifelong learning and further professional development.

Graduate Attributes (GA)

  • Apply fundamental knowledge of mathematics, science, and electronic engineering to solve complex engineering problems.
  • Identify, analyze, and solve electronic engineering problems using principles of mathematics and science.
  • Design and develop solutions for electronic engineering problems considering health, culture, society, and environment.
  • Use research-based knowledge and methods for experiments, data analysis, and interpretation.
  • Apply modern engineering tools to design, model, and simulate electronic systems.
  • Evaluate engineering solutions in terms of social, health, and safety impact.
  • Understand sustainability and environmental responsibility in engineering practices.
  • Apply ethical principles and uphold professional responsibilities in engineering fields.
  • Function effectively as an individual and as part of a multidisciplinary team.
  • Communicate effectively with documentation, presentations, and engineering reports.
  • Apply knowledge of engineering and management principles in project and decision-making roles.
  • Recognize and commit to the importance of lifelong learning for continuous self-development.
