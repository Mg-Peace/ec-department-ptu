Admission Requirements

  • High school graduates specialized in science can apply for engineering disciplines.
  • English proficiency is indispensable. Allocation is made by the academic committee of the University on the basis of merit and choice preferences of each candidate. Therefore, high school grading is obviously important.

Registration Requirements for Freshers

Registration process flow charts are displayed on the University’s Notice Board and in the Student’s Affair Office. For registration, new intake students must submit:

  • A complete application form
  • Transcript or High School Credentials
  • A copy of marks certificate
  • A copy of NRC of parents and applicant
  • 5 pcs of (1.5″ x 1.3″) license photo

Subjects Offered by Year

  • 1st Year: English, Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Chemistry, Engineering Physics, Basic Engineering Drawing, Fundamental of Electronic Circuits
  • 2nd Year: English, Engineering Mathematics, Communication principles, Electronics Engineering Circuit, Digital Electronics, Microelectronics, Technical Programming
  • 3rd Year: English, Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Circuit Analysis, Digital Communication, Engineering Electromagnetic, Integrated Electronics, Modeling and Control
  • 4th Year: English, Engineering Mathematics, Computer Communication, Digital Design with HDL, Modern Control System, Electrical Machines, Industrial Electronic and Control
  • 5th Year: Advanced Electronics, PLC Programming Methods and Techniques, Digital Control System, Digital Signal Processing, Microwave Engineering, Modern Electronic Communication Systems, Industrial Management
  • Final Year (B.E): Humanities and Social Science, Computer Networking
  • M.E: English, Engineering Mathematics, Advanced Electronic, Digital Signal Processing, Data Communication Network, Digital Control System, Advanced Control System
